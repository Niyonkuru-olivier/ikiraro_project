Ongoing Agriculture Services and Projects

Purpose

At IKIRARO, we are committed to providing innovative agricultural services aimed at enhancing the maize farming sector. Our focus is on modern farming techniques, crop management, and the sustainable use of fertilizers.

Maize Crop Services

In maize farming, proper use of fertilizers is crucial. We focus on three main types of fertilizers:

  • NPK
  • DAP
  • Imborera (organic fertilizer)

Farming Technologies in Maize Agriculture

We are embracing modern technologies to improve maize productivity, including:

  • Precision Farming
  • Drone Technology for field monitoring
  • Automated Irrigation Systems

These technologies help farmers manage their crops more effectively and sustainably, reducing resource waste and increasing yields.
